FAQ

Common questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
How do I update my profile details? To update your profile details, click on the 'Edit' button in the description section.
How can I add tasks to my profile? To add tasks to your profile, click on the 'Add Task' button and fill in the required details.
Can I upload multiple images for my profile? Yes, you can upload multiple images for your profile. Simply click on the 'Upload Image' button and select the images you want to upload.
How do I start the timer for a task? To start the timer for a task, click on the 'Start Timer' button next to the respective task.
How can I connect with other users? To connect with other users, go to the 'Connected Users' section and click on the name of the service provider you want to connect with.
